About

Research, sound and software

I am a musician and sound technician focused on digital signal processing, computer music and experimental sound design. My work investigates the relationship between technical systems, timbre, perception and musical practice.

This portfolio collects my audio software, academic projects, electroacoustic works and research-oriented tools.

  • Education Sound Technology, Conservatorio “O. Respighi”
  • Tools Max/MSP, RNBO, Faust, Python, C++
  • Interests DSP, psychoacoustics, synthesis, spatial audio
  • Based in Italy
